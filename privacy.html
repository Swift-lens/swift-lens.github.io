Privacy & Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Effective immediately
The short version: SwiftLens does not sell your data, does not store your screenshots, and does not track you. Your screen captures are processed by third-party AI services and immediately discarded.

1. Information We Collect

When you use SwiftLens, we collect minimal information necessary to operate the service:

  • License key — used to validate your subscription against our server.
  • Telegram chat ID — stored locally on your device to deliver AI responses to your Telegram account.
  • Server heartbeat data — your license key is sent to our server periodically to verify your subscription is still active. We log the timestamp but not the content of your queries.
  • Download portal — we log your IP address and user agent when you request a download token, purely for rate-limiting and abuse prevention. This data is deleted after 7 days.

2. Screenshots and AI Queries

When you press Alt+S or Alt+T, SwiftLens captures your screen and sends it to one of the following AI providers:

  • Google Gemini — governed by Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service
  • Groq (Llama 4) — governed by Groq's Privacy Policy
  • Phi-3 Mini (offline) — runs entirely on your device. Nothing is sent anywhere.

Screenshots are transmitted over HTTPS and are not stored by SwiftLens on any server. Third-party AI providers may process images according to their own policies — we recommend reviewing their terms if this is a concern.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To validate your subscription and grant access to the application
  • To deliver AI-generated responses to your Telegram account
  • To prevent abuse of the download system
  • To respond to support requests you submit through our website

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the SwiftLens service.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties except:

  • AI providers (Google, Groq) who process your screen captures to generate responses
  • Supabase — our database provider, used to store license information
  • Cloudflare — used for file delivery and bot protection on the download portal

5. Data Storage and Security

Your license key and configuration are stored locally on your device in AES-256-GCM encrypted files, derived from a machine-specific key. They cannot be read by other users or applications on your system.

Our servers use industry-standard security practices. License data is stored in Supabase with Row Level Security enabled, preventing any direct public access.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your license record at any time by contacting support. Deleting your record will immediately revoke access to the application.

7. Contact

If you have privacy concerns, please contact us through the Support page.
